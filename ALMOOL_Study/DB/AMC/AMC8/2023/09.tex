Malaika is skiing on a mountain. The graph below shows her elevation, in meters, above the base of the mountain as she skis along a trail. In total, how many seconds does she spend at an elevation between 4 and 7 meters?

\begin{center}		
    \includegraphics[scale=0.4]{AMC-8-pics/2023-09.png}
	\end{center}

\ansFIVEs{%start
		6 }{%<--- (A)
		8 }{%<--- (B)
		10 }{%<--- (C)
		 12}{%<---- (D)
		 14}%<---- (D)
}\SetValue{Concept}{%

말라이카는 산에서 스키를 타고 있습니다. 아래 그래프는 그녀가 트레일을 따라 스키를 타는 동안 산 기슭의 고도를 미터 단위로 보여줍니다. 총 몇 초 동안 4미터에서 7미터 사이의 고도에 머물렀을까요?

}\SetValue{AltText}{%



}\SetValue{Solution}{%

We mark the time intervals in which Malaika's elevation is between $4$ and $7$ meters in red, as shown below:

\begin{center}		
    \includegraphics[scale=0.6]{AMC-8-pics/2023-09-s.png}
	\end{center}

The requested time intervals are:

- from the 2 nd to the 4 th seconds

- from the 6 th to the 10 th seconds

- from the 12 th to the 14 th seconds
	
In total, Malaika spends $(4-2)+(10-6)+(14-12)=(\mathrm{B}) 8$ seconds at such elevation.
