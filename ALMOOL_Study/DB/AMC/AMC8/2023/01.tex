What is the value of $(8 \times 4+2)-(8+4 \times 2)$?

\ansFIVEs{%start

}\SetValue{Solution}{%

\begin{align*}
	(8 \times 4 + 2) - (8 + 4 \times 2) 
	&= (32 + 2) - (8 + 8) \\
	&= 34 - 16 \\
	&= 18
	\end{align*}
	
	(D) 18.
