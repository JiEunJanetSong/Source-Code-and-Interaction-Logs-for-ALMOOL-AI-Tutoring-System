Fifteen integers $a_1, a_2, a_3, \ldots, a_{15}$ are arranged in order on a number line. The integers are equally spaced and have the property that

$$
1 \leq a_1 \leq 10,13 \leq a_2 \leq 20, \text { and } 241 \leq a_{15} \leq 250
$$

What is the sum of digits of $a_{14}$?

\ansFIVEs{%start
		8 }{%<--- (A)
		9 }{%<--- (B)
		10 }{%<--- (C)
		11 }{%<---- (D)
		12 }%<---- (D)
}\SetValue{Concept}{%



}\SetValue{AltText}{%



}\SetValue{Solution}{%

We are given that $a_1, a_2, \ldots, a_{15}$ form an arithmetic sequence.

Given:

- $1 \leq a_1 \leq 10$

- $13 \leq a_2=a_1+d \leq 20$

- $241 \leq a_{15}=a_1+14 d \leq 250$

Step 1: Find possible $d$

From the third condition:

$$
\begin{aligned}
241 \leq a_1+14 d & \leq 250 \Rightarrow 231 \leq 14 d \leq 249 \quad\left(\text { since } a_1 \leq 10\right) \\
\Rightarrow & 16.5 \leq d \leq 17.79 \Rightarrow d=17
\end{aligned}
$$


Step 2: Find possible $a_1$

From the second condition:

$$
13 \leq a_1+17 \leq 20 \Rightarrow-4 \leq a_1 \leq 3
$$


Combine with $1 \leq a_1 \leq 10$ :

$$
1 \leq a_1 \leq 3
$$


Try values:

- $a_1=3 \Rightarrow a_{15}=3+14 \cdot 17=241$

Step 3: Find $a_{14}$

$$
a_{14}=a_1+13 d=3+13 \cdot 17=224
$$


Step 4: Digit sum

$$
2+2+4=8
$$


Answer: (A) 8
