Four friends do yardwork for their neighbors over the weekend, earning $\$ 15, \$ 20, \$ 25$, and $\$ 40$, respectively. They decide to split their earnings equally among themselves. In total how much will the friend who earned $\$ 40$ give to the others?

\ansFIVEs{%start
		\$ 5 }{%<--- (A)
		\$ 10 }{%<--- (B)
		\$ 15 }{%<--- (C)
		\$ 20 }{%<---- (D)
		\$ 25 }%<---- (D)

}\SetValue{Concept}{%



}\SetValue{AltText}{%



}\SetValue{Solution}{%


Altogether the four friends earned $\$ 100$, so they each will have $\$ 25$ after they divide their earnings equally. The friend who earned $\$ 40$ will give to the others $\$ 40-\$ 25=\$ 15$.
