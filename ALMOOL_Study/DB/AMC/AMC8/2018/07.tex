The 5-digit number $\underline{2} \underline{0} \underline{1} \underline{8} \underline{U}$ is divisible by 9. What is the remainder when this number is divided by 8?

\ansFIVEs{%start
		1 }{%<--- (A)
		3 }{%<--- (B)
		5 }{%<--- (C)
		6 }{%<---- (D)
		7 }%<---- (D)
}\SetValue{Concept}{%



}\SetValue{AltText}{%



}\SetValue{Solution}{%

Answer (B):
To be divisible by 9 , the sum of the digits must be divisible by 9, so $2+0+1+8+U=11+U$ is divisible by 9. Thus $U=7$, and the 5 -digit number is 20187. Then because $20187=8 \cdot 2523+3$, the remainder is 3.
