The Math Team designed a logo shaped like a multiplication symbol, shown below on a grid of 1-inch squares. What is the area of the logo in square inches?

\begin{center}		
    \includegraphics[scale=0.6]{AMC-8-pics/2022-01.png}
	\end{center}

\ansFIVEs{%start
		 10 }{%<--- (A)
		 12 }{%<--- (B)
		 13 }{%<--- (C)
		 14 }{%<---- (D)
		 15 }%<---- (D)
}\SetValue{Concept}{%



}\SetValue{AltText}{%



}\SetValue{Solution}{%

Step-by-step:

- The logo is made of squares on a 1-inch grid.

- Count:

- 4 full squares $\rightarrow 4 \times 1=4$ square inches

- 12 half squares $\rightarrow 12 \times \frac{1}{2}=6$ square inches

Total Area:

$4+6=10$ square inches

Answer: (A) 10
