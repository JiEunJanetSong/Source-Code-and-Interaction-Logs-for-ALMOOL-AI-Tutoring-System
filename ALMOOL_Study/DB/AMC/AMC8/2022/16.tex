Four numbers are written in a row. The average of the first two is 21, the average of the middle two is 26, and the average of the last two is 30. What is the average of the first and last of the numbers?

\ansFIVEs{%start
		 24 }{%<--- (A)
		 25 }{%<--- (B)
		 26 }{%<--- (C)
		 27 }{%<---- (D)
		 28 }%<---- (D)
		 
}\SetValue{Concept}{%

네 개의 숫자가 연속으로 적혀 있습니다. 처음 두 숫자의 평균은 21, 중간 두 숫자의 평균은 26, 마지막 두 숫자의 평균은 30입니다. 숫자의 첫 번째와 마지막 숫자의 평균은 얼마인가요?

}\SetValue{AltText}{%



}\SetValue{Solution}{%

We are given:

$$
\begin{aligned}
& \frac{a+b}{2}=21 \\
& \frac{b+c}{2}=26 \\
& \frac{c+d}{2}=30
\end{aligned}
$$


We are asked to find:

$$
\frac{a+d}{2}
$$


Step 1: Add equations (1) and (3)

$$
\frac{a+b}{2}+\frac{c+d}{2}=21+30=51 \Rightarrow \frac{a+b+c+d}{2}=51
$$


Step 2: Subtract equation (2)

$$
\frac{a+b+c+d}{2}-\frac{b+c}{2}=51-26=25 \Rightarrow \frac{a+d}{2}=25
$$


Answer: (B) 25
