The figure below shows a polygon $A B C D E F G H$, consisting of rectangles and right triangles. When cut out and folded on the dotted lines, the polygon forms a triangular prism. Suppose that $A H=E F=8$ and $G H=14$. What is the volume of the prism?

}\SetValue{Concept}{%

아래 그림은 직사각형과 직각 삼각형으로 구성된 다각형 $A -B-C-D -E-F-G -H$를 보여줍니다. 점선으로 잘라내어 접으면 다각형은 삼각형 프리즘을 형성합니다. A-H=E- F=8과 -$G-H=14$라고 가정합니다. 프리즘의 부피는 어떻게 될까요?

}\SetValue{Solution}{%

Mathematical Solution

We are given a triangular prism formed by folding a 2D net. To find its volume, we use the prism volume formula:

$$
\text { Volume }=\text { Area of base } \times \text { Height }
$$


Step 1: Identify the base triangle

After folding, triangle $\triangle B J G$ becomes one of the triangular bases.

We are told:

- $B J=C I=8$

- $G H=14$, and since $H J=8$, it follows that:

$$
J G=G H-H J=14-8=6
$$


Thus, triangle $B J G$ has:

- Base $=B J=8$

- Height $=J G=6$

$$
\text { Area }_{\triangle B J G}=\frac{1}{2} \cdot 8 \cdot 6=24
$$


Step 2: Prism height

The prism's height is the length perpendicular to the triangular base, which corresponds to the rectangle $F G$, i.e.,

$$
\text { Height }=F G=8
$$


Step 3: Volume

$$
\text { Volume }=\text { Area }_{\triangle B J G} \cdot \text { Height }=24 \cdot 8=192
$$


Answer: (C) 192.
