Consider these two operations:

$$
\begin{aligned}
a \diamond b & =a^2-b^2 \\
a \star b & =(a-b)^2
\end{aligned}
$$


What is the value of $(5 \diamond 3) \star 6 ?$

		 -20 }{%<--- (A)

}\SetValue{Solution}{%

Given operations:

$$
\begin{gathered}
a \diamond b=a^2-b^2 \\
a \star b=(a-b)^2
\end{gathered}
$$


We are asked to evaluate:

$$
(5 \diamond 3) \star 6
$$


Step 1: Compute $5 \diamond 3$

$$
5 \diamond 3=5^2-3^2=25-9=16
$$


Step 2: Compute $16 \star 6$

$$
16 \star 6=(16-6)^2=10^2=100
$$


Final Answer:

$$
100
$$


Answer: (D) 100
