Henry the donkey has a very long piece of pasta. He takes a number of bites of pasta, each time eating 3 inches of pasta from the middle of one piece. In the end, he has 10 pieces of pasta whose total length is 17 inches. How long, in inches, was the piece of pasta he started with?

\ansFIVEs{%start
		 34 }{%<--- (A)
		 38 }{%<--- (B)
		 41 }{%<--- (C)
		 44 }{%<---- (D)
		 47 }%<---- (D)
		 
}\SetValue{Concept}{%

당나귀 헨리는 아주 긴 파스타 조각을 가지고 있어요. 헨리는 파스타를 여러 번 먹는데, 한 조각의 중간부터 3인치씩 먹습니다. 결국 헨리는 총 길이가 17인치인 파스타 조각 10개를 먹게 됩니다. 그가 처음 먹기 시작한 파스타 조각의 길이는 몇 인치였을까요?

}\SetValue{AltText}{%



}\SetValue{Solution}{%

We are told:

- Henry takes bites of 3 inches from the middle of one pasta piece.

- Each bite increases the number of pieces by 1.

- He ends with 10 pieces and a total length of 17 inches.

- We are to find the original length of the pasta.

Step 1: Let $x$ be the number of bites

- Start with 1 piece.

- Each bite adds 1 piece $\rightarrow$

$$
1+x=10 \Rightarrow x=9
$$


Step 2: Total pasta removed

- Each bite removes 3 inches $\rightarrow$

$$
3 \times 9=27 \text { inches eaten }
$$


Step 3: Add back to get original length

- Remaining = 17 inches

- Original $=27+17=44$

Answer: (D) 44
