If $n$ is an even positive integer, the double factorial notation $n$ !! represents the product of all the even integers from 2 to $n$. For example, $8!!=2 \cdot 4 \cdot 6 \cdot 8$. What is the units digit of the following sum?

$$
2!!+4!!+6!!+\cdots+2018!!+2020!!+2022!!
$$

\ansFIVEs{%start
		0 }{%<--- (A)
		2 }{%<--- (B)
		4 }{%<--- (C)
		6 }{%<---- (D)
		8 }%<---- (D)
		
}\SetValue{Concept}{%

n이 짝수 양의 정수인 경우, 이중 인수분해 표기법 $n$ !! 은 2에서 $n$까지의 모든 짝수 정수의 곱을 나타냅니다. 예를 들어, $8!!=2 \cdot 4 \cdot 6 \cdot 8$입니다. 다음 합계의 단위 자릿수는 무엇인가요?

}\SetValue{AltText}{%



}\SetValue{Solution}{%

Key idea:

- For even $n, n!!=2 \cdot 4 \cdot 6 \cdots n$

- Once $n \geq 10, n$ !! includes both a factor of 2 and $5 
\rightarrow$ product divisible by 10 $\rightarrow$ units digit is 0

So only need to compute:

$$
2!!+4!!+6!!+8!!
$$


Compute each:

- $2!!=2$

- $4!!=2 \cdot 4=8$

- $6!!=2 \cdot 4 \cdot 6=48$

- $8!!=2 \cdot 4 \cdot 6 \cdot 8=384$

- $2018!!=2 \cdot 4 \cdot 6 \cdot 8 \cdot 10 \cdots 2018=0$

- $2020!!=2 \cdot 4 \cdot 6 \cdot 8 \cdot 10 \cdots 2020=0$

- $2022!!=2 \cdot 4 \cdot 6 \cdot 8 \cdot 10 \cdots 2022=0$

Now, units digits:

- $2!!=2$

- $4!!=8$

- $6!!=8$

- $8!!=4$

Sum of units digits:

$$
2+8+8+4=22 \Rightarrow 2
$$


Answer: (B) 2
