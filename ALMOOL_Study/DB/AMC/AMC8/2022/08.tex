We are asked to evaluate:

$$
\frac{1}{3} \cdot \frac{2}{4} \cdot \frac{3}{5} \cdots \frac{18}{20} \cdot \frac{19}{21} \cdot \frac{20}{22}
$$


Step 1: Recognize the pattern

This is a product of 20 fractions of the form:

$$
\frac{n}{n+2}, \quad \text { for } n=1 \text { to } 20
$$


So the full product is:

$$
\frac{1 \cdot 2 \cdot 3 \cdots 20}{3 \cdot 4 \cdot 5 \cdots 22}=\frac{20!}{22!/(1 \cdot 2)}=\frac{20!\cdot 2}{22!}
$$


Step 2: Simplify

$$
\frac{20!\cdot 2}{22 \cdot 21 \cdot 20!}=\frac{2}{22 \cdot 21}=\frac{2}{462}=\frac{1}{231}
$$


Answer: (B) $\frac{1}{231}$
