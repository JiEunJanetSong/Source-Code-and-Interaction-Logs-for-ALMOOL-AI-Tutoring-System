What is the value of the expression $\sqrt{16 \sqrt{8 \sqrt{4}}}$?

\ansFIVEs{%start
		4 }{%<--- (A)
		4 \sqrt{2} }{%<--- (B)
		8 }{%<--- (C)
		8 \sqrt{2} }{%<---- (D)
		16 }%<---- (D)
}\SetValue{Concept}{%



}\SetValue{AltText}{%



}\SetValue{Solution}{%

Answer (C): Simplifying yields

$$
\sqrt{16 \sqrt{8 \sqrt{4}}}=\sqrt{16 \sqrt{16}}=\sqrt{64}=8
$$
