Alicia, Brenda, and Colby were the candidates in a recent election for student president. The pie chart below shows how the votes were distributed among the three candidates. If Brenda received 36 votes, then how many votes were cast all together?

\begin{center}		
    \includegraphics[scale=0.6]{AMC-8-pics/2017-02.png}
	\end{center}

\ansFIVEs{%start
		70 }{%<--- (A)
		84 }{%<--- (B)
		100 }{%<--- (C)
		106 }{%<---- (D)
		120 }%<---- (D)
}\SetValue{Concept}{%



}\SetValue{AltText}{%



}\SetValue{Solution}{%

Answer (E): Observe that 36 votes made up $30 \%$ of the total number of votes. Thus 12 votes made up $10 \%$ of the total number of votes and therefore there were 120 total votes.
