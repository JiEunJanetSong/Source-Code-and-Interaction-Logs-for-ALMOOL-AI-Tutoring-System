When 0.000315 is multiplied by $7,928,564$ the product is closest to which of the following?

\ansFIVEs{%start
		210 }{%<--- (A)
		240 }{%<--- (B)
		2,100 }{%<--- (C)
		2,400 }{%<---- (D)
		24,000 }%<---- (D)
}\SetValue{Concept}{%



}\SetValue{AltText}{%



}\SetValue{Solution}{%

Answer (D): The product may be estimated as

$$
\left(3 \cdot 10^{-4}\right)\left(8 \cdot 10^6\right)=24 \cdot 10^2=2400
$$


The exact value of the product is 2497.498.
