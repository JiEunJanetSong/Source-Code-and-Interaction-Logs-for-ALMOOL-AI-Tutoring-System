In the arrangement of letters and numerals below, by how many different paths can one spell $A M C 8$? Beginning at the $A$ in the middle, a path allows only moves from one letter to an adjacent (above, below, left, or right, but not diagonal) letter. One example of such a path is traced in the picture.

\begin{center}		
    \includegraphics[scale=0.6]{AMC-8-pics/2017-15.png}
	\end{center}

\ansFIVEs{%start
		8 }{%<--- (A)
		9 }{%<--- (B)
		12 }{%<--- (C)
		24 }{%<---- (D)
		36 }%<---- (D)
}\SetValue{Concept}{%



}\SetValue{AltText}{%



}\SetValue{Solution}{%

Answer (D): Starting at $A$, there are 4 choices for the $M$, each of which is followed by 3 choices for the $C$, each of which is followed by 2 choices for the 8. So all together, there are $4 \cdot 3 \cdot 2=24$ paths.
