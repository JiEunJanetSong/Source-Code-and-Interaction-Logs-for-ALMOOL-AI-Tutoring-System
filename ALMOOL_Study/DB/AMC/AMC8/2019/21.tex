What is the area of the triangle formed by the lines $y=5, y=1+x$, and $y=1-x$?

	\ansFIVEs{%start
		 4}{%<--- (A)
		 8 }{%<--- (B)
		 10 }{%<--- (C)
		 12 }{%<---- (D)
	 16}%<---- (D)

}\SetValue{Concept}{%



}\SetValue{AltText}{%



}\SetValue{Solution}{%

These three lines enclose a triangle whose vertices are $(0,1),(4,5)$, and $(-4,5)$. The base of this triangle on the line $y=5$ has length 8, and its altitude on the $y$-axis has length 4. Thus the area of the triangle is $\frac{1}{2} \cdot 8 \cdot 4=16$.
