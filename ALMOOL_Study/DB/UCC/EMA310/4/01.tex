Find the angle between the vectors $a=2 i+2 j-k$ and $b=5 i-3 j+2 k$



}\SetValue{Concept}{%



}\SetValue{AltText}{%



}\SetValue{Solution}{%

To find the angle $\theta$ between two vectors $\vec{a}$ and $\vec{b}$, we use the dot product formula:

$$
\vec{a} \cdot \vec{b}=|\vec{a}||\vec{b}| \cos \theta
$$


Step 1: Compute the dot product $\vec{a} \cdot \vec{b}$
Given:

$$
\begin{gathered}
\vec{a}=2 i+2 j-k, \quad \vec{b}=5 i-3 j+2 k \\
\vec{a} \cdot \vec{b}=(2)(5)+(2)(-3)+(-1)(2)=10-6-2=2
\end{gathered}
$$


Step 2: Find magnitudes of $\vec{a}$ and $\vec{b}$

$$
\begin{gathered}
|\vec{a}|=\sqrt{2^2+2^2+(-1)^2}=\sqrt{4+4+1}=\sqrt{9}=3 \\
|\vec{b}|=\sqrt{5^2+(-3)^2+2^2}=\sqrt{25+9+4}=\sqrt{38}
\end{gathered}
$$


Step 3: Solve for $\cos \theta$

$$
\cos \theta=\frac{\vec{a} \cdot \vec{b}}{|\vec{a}||\vec{b}|}=\frac{2}{3 \sqrt{38}}
$$


Step 4: Compute $\theta$

$$
\theta=\cos ^{-1}\left(\frac{2}{3 \sqrt{38}}\right)
$$


Approximating:

- $\sqrt{38} \approx 6.16$

- $3 \sqrt{38} \approx 18.48$

- $\cos \theta \approx \frac{2}{18.48} \approx 0.1082$

$$
\theta \approx \cos ^{-1}(0.1082) \approx 83.79^{\circ}
$$


Final Answer:

$$
\theta \approx 83.8^{\circ}
$$
