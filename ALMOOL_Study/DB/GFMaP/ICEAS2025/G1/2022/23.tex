A closed cylinder has height $10\mathrm{cm}$ and diameter $7\mathrm{cm}$. Find the total surface area of the cylinder. [Take $\pi=\dfrac{22}{7}$, Round and express as a whole number.]

}\SetValue{Translation}{%

}\SetValue{Hint}{%



}\SetValue{GPTSolution}{%

}\SetValue{AltText}{%



}\SetValue{Solution}{%

We have:

- Height $h = 10 \text{ cm}$ 

- Diameter $d = 7 \text{ cm} \implies \text{radius }r = \frac{7}{2} = 3.5 \text{ cm}$ 

- Total surface area $= 2\pi r^2 + 2\pi r h$.
  
- Use $\pi = \tfrac{22}{7}$.

Step-by-step:

1. Compute $2\pi r^2$:  
$$
2\pi r^2 
= 2 \times \frac{22}{7} \times (3.5)^2 
= \frac{44}{7} \times 12.25 
= \frac{44 \times 12.25}{7}
= \frac{539}{7}
= 77.
$$

2. Compute $2\pi r h$:  
$$
2\pi r h 
= 2 \times \frac{22}{7} \times 3.5 \times 10 
= \frac{44}{7} \times 3.5 \times 10 
= \frac{44 \times 3.5}{7} \times 10 
= \frac{154}{7} \times 10 
= 22 \times 10 
= 220.
$$

3. Total surface area:  
$$
\text{Surface area} = 77 + 220 = 297 \text{ cm}^2.
$$

Hence, the total surface area of the cylinder is $297\text{ cm}^2$.

}\SetValue{Answer}{%

297
