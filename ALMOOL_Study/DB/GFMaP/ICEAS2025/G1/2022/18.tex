Find the size of an interior angle of a regular nonagon.

}\SetValue{Translation}{%

}\SetValue{Hint}{%



}\SetValue{GPTSolution}{%



}\SetValue{AltText}{%



}\SetValue{Solution}{%

A nonagon is a nine-sided polygon (also called a 9-gon).  

To find the size of an interior angle of a regular nonagon, you can use the formula:

$$
\text{Interior Angle} = \frac{(n-2) \times 180}{n}
$$

where $n$ is the number of sides.

For a nonagon ($n = 9$):

$$
\text{Interior Angle} = \frac{(9-2) \times 180}{9} = \frac{7 \times 180}{9} = \frac{1260}{9} = 140^\circ
$$

So, each interior angle of a regular nonagon is 140 degrees.

}\SetValue{Answer}{%

140
