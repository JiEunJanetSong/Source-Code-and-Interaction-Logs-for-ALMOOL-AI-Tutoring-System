The sum of the angles of a regular polygon is $2520^{\circ}$. How many sides does the polygon have?

}\SetValue{Translation}{%

}\SetValue{Hint}{%



}\SetValue{GPTSolution}{%

}\SetValue{AltText}{%



}\SetValue{Solution}{%

The sum of the interior angles $S$ of an $n$-sided polygon is given by

$$
S = (n - 2)\times 180^\circ.
$$

We are told $S = 2520^\circ$. 

Hence,
$$
(n - 2)\times 180^\circ = 2520^\circ 
\quad \Longrightarrow \quad 
n - 2 = \frac{2520}{180} 
= 14 
\quad \Longrightarrow \quad 
n = 16.
$$

Therefore, the polygon has $\boxed{16}$ sides.

}\SetValue{Answer}{%

16
