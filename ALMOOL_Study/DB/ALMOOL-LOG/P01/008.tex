\begin{GPTQ}
    How to find volume of sphere with 34 inches of radius   
\end{GPTQ}

\begin{GPTA}
 To find the volume of a sphere with a radius of 34 inches, you can use the sphere volume formula:
    
    $$
    V=\frac{4}{3} \pi r^3
    $$
    
    where $V$ is the volume, and $r$ is the radius of the sphere.

    Steps:

    1. Identify the Radius: The given radius $r$ is 34 inches.

    2. Substitute into the Formula:
    
    $$
    V=\frac{4}{3} \pi(34)^3
    $$
    
    3. Calculate $34^3$ : 
    
    Compute $34^3$ :
    
    $$
    34^3=34 \times 34 \times 34=39,304
    $$
    
    4. Plug Back into the Formula:
    
    $$
    V=\frac{4}{3} \pi \times 39,304
    $$
    
    5. Multiply by $\frac{4}{3}$ :

    $$
    V=\frac{4}{3} \times 39,304 \approx 52,405.3333
    $$
    
    6. Multiply by $\pi$ :
    
    $$
    V \approx 52,405.3333 \times \pi \approx 52,405.3333 \times 3.14159 \approx 164,636 \text { cubic inches }
    $$
    
    
    Therefore, the volume of the sphere with a radius of 34 inches is approximately 164,636 cubic inches.
\end{GPTA}
