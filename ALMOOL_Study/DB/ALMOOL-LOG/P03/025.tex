\begin{GPTA}
    The formula to calculate the height of the whole cone $(h)$ from the height of the frustrum $(H)$ and consid ering the radius ratio (where the bottom radius is three times the top radius) is:
    
    $$
    h=H+\frac{H}{2}
    $$
    
    
    Which simplifies in given knowns to:
    
    $$
    h=\frac{3}{2} H
    $$
    
    
    For this specific case, with $H=12 \mathrm{~cm}$ :
    
    $$
    h=\frac{3}{2} \times 12=18 \mathrm{~cm}
    $$
    
        
\end{GPTA}
