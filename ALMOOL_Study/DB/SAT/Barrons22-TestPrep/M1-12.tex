$$
2(a-4 b)\left(3+b^3\right)=?
$$

\ansFOURs{%start
2 a b^3-24 b}{%<--- (A)
2 a b+6 a-4 b^2-12 b}{%<--- (B)
2 a b^3+4 a-6 b^4-24 b}{%<--- (C)
2 a b^3+6 a-8 b^4-24 b}%<---- (D)

}\SetValue{Solution}{%

Answer: \textbf{(D)} 

Use FOIL to calculate the product of the terms in parentheses:

$
2(a-4 b)\left(3+b^3\right)=2\left(3 a+a b^3-12 b-4 b^4\right)=6 a+2 a b^3-24 b-8 b^4
$

Rearrange the values to put them in the order shown in the choices:

$2 a b^3+6 a-8 b^4-24 b$

}\SetValue{Answer}{%
D
