If $\frac{1}{4} a+\frac{1}{3} b=2$, what is the value of $3 a+4 b$ ?

\ansFOURs{%start
4}{%<--- (A)
18}{%<--- (B)
24}{%<--- (C)
36}%<---- (D)

}\SetValue{Solution}{%
Answer: \textbf{(C)} 

We are given the equation:

$
\frac{1}{4} a + \frac{1}{3} b = 2
$

We need to determine the value of $3a + 4b$.

 Step 1: Eliminate Fractions  
Multiply the entire equation by 12 (the least common multiple of 4 and 3) to clear the fractions:

$
12 \times \left( \frac{1}{4} a + \frac{1}{3} b \right) = 12 \times 2
$

$
3a + 4b = 24
$

 Step 2: Select the Correct Answer  
The correct choice is $C) 24$.
