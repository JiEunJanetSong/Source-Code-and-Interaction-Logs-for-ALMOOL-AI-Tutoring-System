If the diameter of a circle is 10 centimeters, what is the circumference of the circle in centimeters?

}\SetValue{Solution}{%

Answer: $31.41$ or $31.42$. 

To solve for the circumference of a circle, multiply the diameter by pi: $\pi \times 10 \approx 31.41593$, which can be entered on the fill-in as 31.41 or 31.42.

}\SetValue{Answer}{%
31.41, 31.42
