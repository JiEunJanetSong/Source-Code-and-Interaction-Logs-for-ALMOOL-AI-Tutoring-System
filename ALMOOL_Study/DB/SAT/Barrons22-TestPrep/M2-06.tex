$$
\left(x^2+3 x^2\right)^3=?
$$

\ansFOURs{%start
3 x^{12 x}}{%<--- (A)
27 x^5}{%<--- (B)
28 x^6}{%<--- (C)
64 x^6}%<---- (D)

}\SetValue{Solution}{%
Answer: \textbf{(D)} 

Simplify by combining the like terms within the parentheses:

$
\left(x^2+3 x^2\right)^3=\left(4 x^2\right)^3
$


Next, cube both the 4 and the $x^2$ :

$
\left(4 x^2\right)^3 \rightarrow\left(4^3\right)\left(x^2\right)^3 \rightarrow 64 x^6
$

}\SetValue{Answer}{%
D
