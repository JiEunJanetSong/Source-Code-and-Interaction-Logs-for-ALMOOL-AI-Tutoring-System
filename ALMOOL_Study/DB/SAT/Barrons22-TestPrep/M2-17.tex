A cube has edges with a length of 2 inches. What is the surface area of the cube in square inches?

}\SetValue{Solution}{%
Answer: $24$

 There are six faces to a cube, each of which will have the same edge length of 2 inches. Find the area of one of these faces by multiplying the length of the face by the width:. Then, multiply 4 by 6 to get the total area of all the cube's faces: $4 \times 6=24$.
}\SetValue{Answer}{%
24
