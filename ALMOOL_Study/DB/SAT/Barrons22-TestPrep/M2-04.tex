If $n$ represents $m$ percent of $x$, how could the value of $n$ be calculated?

\ansFOURs{%start
n=\frac{m x}{100}}{%<--- (A)
n=0.1 m x}{%<--- (B)
n=\frac{100}{m x}}{%<--- (C)
n=100 m x}%<---- (D)

}\SetValue{Solution}{%
Answer: \textbf{(A)} 

We are given that $ n $ represents $ m $ percent of $ x $. This means:

$
n = \frac{m}{100} \times x
$

 Step 1: Convert Percent to Fraction  
Since $ m $ percent means $ \frac{m}{100} $ in decimal form, we write:

$
n = \frac{m x}{100}
$

 Step 2: Select the Correct Answer  
The correct choice is $ A) n = \frac{m x}{100} $.


}\SetValue{Answer}{%
A
