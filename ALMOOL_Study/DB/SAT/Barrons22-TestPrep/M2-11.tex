The following equations could all have the constant $k$ equal zero and have a defined solution EXCEPT

\ansFOURs{%start
k x=0}{%<--- (A)
3=k-x}{%<--- (B)
4 k+x=7}{%<--- (C)
\frac{2}{k}=x}%<---- (D)

}\SetValue{Solution}{%
Answer: \textbf{(D)} 

$\dfrac{2}{k}=x$ is undefined if $k=0$. Dividing by 0 results in an undefined solution. All of the other equations have a solution for $x$ when $k=0$.
}\SetValue{Answer}{%
D
