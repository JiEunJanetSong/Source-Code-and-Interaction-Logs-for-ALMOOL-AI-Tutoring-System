If $f(x)=7 x+3$ and $g(x)=2 x^2$, what is the value of $f(g(1))$ ?

}\SetValue{Solution}{%

Answer: $17$ 

Work inside out by first solving for $g(1)$ :

$
g(x)=2 x^2 \rightarrow g(1)=2(1)^2=2
$


Then put 2 in for $x$ in the $f(x)$ :

$
f(x)=7 x+3 \rightarrow f(2)=7(2)+3=17
$

}\SetValue{Answer}{%
17
