Calculate the first eight terms of the sequence of partial sums correct to four decimal places. Does it appear that the series is convergent or divergent?

$$\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \dfrac{n}{1+\sqrt{n}}$$

}\SetValue{Concept}{%



}\SetValue{AltText}{%



}\SetValue{Solution}{%

We are given the infinite series:

$$
\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \dfrac{n}{1+\sqrt{n}}
$$


Let:

$$
a_n=\dfrac{n}{1+\sqrt{n}}, \quad s_n=\sum_{k=1}^n a_k
$$


We will compute the first eight terms of the sequence of partial sums, rounding to four decimal places, and analyze whether the series appears to converge or diverge.

Step 1: Compute the individual terms $a_n=\dfrac{n}{1+\sqrt{n}}$

\begin{tabular}{|l|l|l|}
\hline $n$ & $\sqrt{n}$ & $a_n=\dfrac{n}{1+\sqrt{n}}$ \\
\hline 1 & 1.0000 & $\dfrac{1}{1+1}=0.5000$ \\
\hline 2 & 1.4142 & $\dfrac{2}{1+1.4142} \approx 0.8284$ \\
\hline 3 & 1.7321 & $\dfrac{3}{1+1.7321} \approx 1.0980$ \\
\hline 4 & 2.0000 & $\dfrac{4}{1+2}=1.3333$ \\
\hline 5 & 2.2361 & $\dfrac{5}{1+2.2361} \approx 1.5451$ \\
\hline 6 & 2.4495 & $\dfrac{6}{1+2.4495} \approx 1.7391$ \\
\hline 7 & 2.6458 & $\dfrac{7}{1+2.6458} \approx 1.9198$ \\
\hline 8 & 2.8284 & $\dfrac{8}{1+2.8284} \approx 2.0882$ \\
\hline
\end{tabular}

Step 2: Compute the partial sums $s_n=\sum_{k=1}^n a_k$

\begin{tabular}{|l|l|}
\hline $n$ & $s_n$ (rounded to 4 decimals) \\
\hline 1 & 0.5000 \\
\hline 2 & 1.3284 \\
\hline 3 & 2.4264 \\
\hline 4 & 3.7597 \\
\hline 5 & 5.3048 \\
\hline 6 & 7.0439 \\
\hline 7 & 8.9637 \\
\hline 8 & 11.0519 \\
\hline
\end{tabular}

Step 3: Analyze convergence

We observe that:

- The individual terms $a_n$ are increasing slowly, and

- The partial sums $s_n$ are growing quickly without any sign of stabilizing.

Also, we can analyze the term's behavior:

$$
a_n=\dfrac{n}{1+\sqrt{n}}=\dfrac{\sqrt{n} \cdot \sqrt{n}}{1+\sqrt{n}} \approx \sqrt{n} \quad \text { as } n \rightarrow \infty
$$


And since $\sum \sqrt{n}$ diverges, this series also diverges.

Final Answer:

- First 8 partial sums (rounded to 4 decimal places):

$$
0.5000,1.3284,2.4264,3.7597,5.3048,7.0439,8.9637,11.0519
$$

- The series appears to be divergent.
