Calculate the sum of the series $\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} a_n$ whose partial sums are given.

$$s_n=2-3(0.8)^n$$

}\SetValue{Concept}{%



}\SetValue{AltText}{%



}\SetValue{Solution}{%

We are given the expression for the partial sums of a series:

$$
s_n=2-3(0.8)^n
$$


To find the sum of the infinite series $\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} a_n$, we look at the limit of the partial sums as $n \rightarrow \infty$ :

$$
\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} a_n=\lim _{n \rightarrow \infty} s_n
$$


Step 1: Take the limit

$$
\lim _{n \rightarrow \infty} s_n=\lim _{n \rightarrow \infty}\left(2-3(0.8)^n\right)
$$


Since $0.8<1$, we know that $(0.8)^n \rightarrow 0$ as $n \rightarrow \infty$. So:

$$
\lim _{n \rightarrow \infty} s_n=2-3(0)=2
$$


Final Answer:

$$
\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} a_n=2
$$
