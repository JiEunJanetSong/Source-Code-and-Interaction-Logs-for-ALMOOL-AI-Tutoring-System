Determine whether the geometric series is convergent or divergent. If it is convergent, find its sum.

$$2+0.5+0.125+0.03125+\cdots$$


}\SetValue{Concept}{%



}\SetValue{AltText}{%



}\SetValue{Solution}{%

Step 1: Identify the first term $a$ and the common ratio $r$

- First term: $a=2$

- To find the common ratio $r$, divide the second term by the first:

$$
r=\dfrac{0.5}{2}=0.25
$$


Check further:

$$
\dfrac{0.125}{0.5}=0.25, \quad \dfrac{0.03125}{0.125}=0.25
$$

So this is a geometric series with:

- $a=2$

- $r=0.25$

Step 2: Convergence test

A geometric series $\sum a r^{n-1}$ converges if:

$$
|r|<1
$$


Here, $|r|=0.25<1 \rightarrow $ The series converges

Step 3: Use geometric series sum formula

$$
S=\dfrac{a}{1-r}=\dfrac{2}{1-0.25}=\dfrac{2}{0.75}=\dfrac{8}{3}
$$

Final Answer:

- The series is convergent.

- The sum is $\dfrac{8}{3}$.
